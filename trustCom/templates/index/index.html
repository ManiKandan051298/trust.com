About Us

  • ARISE (ASSOCIATION OF RURAL INFRA SERVICES AND EDUCATION) which means to STAND UP for the empowerment of society, founded in 2017, is an NGO in India registered as a Non-Profitable Organisation/ trust in Malli, Srivilliputhur, Tamil Nadu.
  • ARISE closely works with the vulnerable communities and empowers the socially marginalized by providing them livelihood linkages, imparting skills leading to employment, health and education facilities. The focus areas of ARISE are livelihoods, skill development, health and education, rights, green environment, rehabilitation and resettlement and other community development initiatives.
  • ARISE began as a Charitable Trust that pioneered in rejuvenating underprivileged and vulnerable neighborhoods into self-sustaining and developed communities by its Integrated Community Development Programes changing the environment of the neighborhood. ARISE supports to the underdeveloped to obtain government identities, providing them access to public benefit schemes, imparting skills leading to employment, providing access to habitable environment and basic education
